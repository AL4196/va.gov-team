# Proposed Global VA Health Service Website Taxonomy
2019 work

## Summary
The VA Health Services Taxonomy represents the result of an an exploration of the A-Z Services Listings of 145 VA facilities websites in addition to review and mappings of additional existing VHA services classifications including:
